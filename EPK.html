Post-Hardcore / Metal — Atlanta, GA

About

Forged in the heavy underground of Atlanta, Palaces blend the emotional weight of post-hardcore with the crushing force of modern metal. Their sound is a collision of towering riffs, serrated rhythms, and raw catharsis — balancing chaos and atmosphere with surgical precision. Known for intense live performances, Palaces craft music that hits as hard psychologically as it does physically.
